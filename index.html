Strategically located along the Mumbai-Bangalore Highway, this project offers unparalleled accessibility for daily commuters, making your commute a breeze. But it’s not just about the location; it’s about the lifestyle that comes with it.

Imagine having a plethora of shopping centers, schools, and colleges just minutes away from your home a convenience that is especially vital for families. With excellent public transport options at your doorstep, getting around the city, whether for work or leisure, is effortless.

What truly sets Prasiddhi Icon apart is its proximity to key economic hubs like the IT parks and MIDC. Moreover, the value of properties in this thriving locality is on the rise, positioning your investment for potential appreciation over time.
